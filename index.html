Intro

Bike gone?
  • The Fietsdepot (Bicycle Depository) receives all bicycles that have been found or removed by the City District officials (City of Amsterdam) or the police
  • Because they were parked in time-limited parking locations or other reasons, for instance if they seem 'abandoned' bikes
Is my bike at the Fietsdepot?
  • To find out if your bicycle is at the Bicycle Depository please either fill out the form
  • Or call +31203344522
Location and Opening hours
  • Location: Bornhout 8, Amsterdam
  • Mon to Fri 08.30 till 17.00
  • Sat 09.00 till 12.30
  • From 6 april to 28 september thursdays open till 20:00

Costs

  • To pick up your bike, you pay a fee of € 22.50

  • Home delivery costs € 35.00

  • You must show a valid ID and keys of the bike locks

  • You can only pay with pin and creditcard
